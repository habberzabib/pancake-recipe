PANCAKE RECIPE

WELCOME! YOU Have Been Chosen! In Just A Few Simple Steps You Too Will Be Able To Manufacture The Ultimate Pancakes!

SERVING SIZE: 4 Small Pancakes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 EGG
  • 1 MILK
  • 1 SUGAR
  • 1 FLOUR
  • 1 BUTTER

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Beat Eggs. Be Sure To Add The Shells For That Extra Crunch.
  2. Drown Eggs In Milk.
  3. Drown Eggs In Milk.
  4. Drown Eggs In Milk.
  5. Dump In The Flour. The Bowl Should Be Overflowing. If Not, You Have Made A Mistake.
  6. Wash Sugar. Thinly Slice Sugar. Add Sugar.
  7. Acquire A Frying Pan.
  8. Break The Frying Pan.
  9. Acquire A Stove.
  10. Turn On The Stove.
  11. Place Butter Atop The Bare Stove Until It Melts.
  12. Pour Pancake Batter.
  13. Repeat The Previous Step Until You Are Finished Using All The Pancake Batter.
